What is rhenium chain
      Rhenium chain is a base chain based on blockchain technology. Its English name is Rhenium chain, which is a public chain similar to Ethereum. It is mainly used in digital trading and intelligent wearable equipment trading. On this rhenium chain, everyone in the world can read and send deals. If they legally have hope to see themselves included. Anyone in the world can participate in the process of consensus formation - decide what blocks to add to the chain and what the status quo is. As an alternative to centralization or quasi-centralization of trust, the public chain is protected by an encrypted economy that is a combination of economic incentives and cryptographic proofs, a mechanism similar to proof of workload or proof of entitlement, with the general principle followed by people The degree to which consensus is formed is proportional to the amount of economic resources they can influence.

Therefore, many applications can only stay in the concept, and can not be applied. As a result, many enterprises are afraid to try, or the cost of failure after trying is high, which causes the experiment not to progress.
